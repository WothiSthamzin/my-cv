About Thami

Thamsanqa Khethe, known to many as Thami, is a Johannesburg-based photographer whose passion and talent for capturing moments are truly remarkable. With a keen understanding that every photo has a story to tell, Thami's work goes beyond simply capturing images; it captures emotions, experiences, and narratives.

Thami, the visionary behind APBT, hails from the vibrant township of Soweto. With a passion for photography, he pursued his formal education in this field at Vega School, Johannesburg Campus. Graduating in 2010, Thami honed his skills and acquired a solid foundation in photography.
